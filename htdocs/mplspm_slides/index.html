OpenGuides

  • A collection of City Guides
  • An Open Source project
    • A wiki with structured metadata
    • A geodata application of Wiki::Toolkit
  • A module on CPAN
  • A community of developers and guide admins
  • OpenGuides homepage http://www.openguides.org
  • CPAN: http://search.cpan.org/dist/OpenGuides/
  • Dev Trac: http://dev.openguides.org
  • IRC Channel #openguides on irc.perl.org

OpenGuides History

  • Started in early 2002 by some London.pm’ers
  • The original Usemod wiki wasn’t good enough
  • The rewrite became OpenGuides, a geographically-aware application of Wiki::Toolkit

Structured Metadata

  • Content is separated from metadata
  • Allows structured searching (all restaurants open at noon within 500m of the Target Center)
  • Easier to contribute, there isn’t just an imposing blank text-area
  • One of the projects for the Twin Cities guide is to get the metadata searching working better.

Geodata

  • US geodata is free
    • Unlike the UK
    • Geocoder.us performs address => longitude and latitude lookup
  • All nodes can have a longitude and latitude
    • Geolocation searching
    • Integrated Google Maps
  • The OpenGuides project has a whole chapter in Mapping Hacks from O’Reilly
