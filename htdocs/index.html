Welcome

Formed in the late 19th century by former officers of the American Union Army, Dahut.pm has become one of the largest, and most enigmatic of the shadowy cartels that pull the strings behind the scenes in the world today. It has been described as the “house is really worth his friends in exceptional cases”.

Dahut.pm is a non-geographical collection of non-cabal people interested in Perl, crypto-zoology, bots, The Semantic Web (with capitals), the semantic web (without capitals), geowanking, SVG, and other erudite philosophies.
